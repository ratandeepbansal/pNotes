# AI and Machine Learning Thoughts

Some random thoughts on the future of AI and its applications.

## Transformers Revolution

The transformer architecture has revolutionized NLP and is now being applied to computer vision, protein folding, and many other domains.

## Local AI Models

Running AI models locally ensures privacy and reduces dependency on cloud services. Tools like Ollama make this accessible.

## Ethical Considerations

As AI becomes more powerful, we need to think carefully about:
- Bias in training data
- Privacy concerns
- Job displacement
- Alignment with human values
